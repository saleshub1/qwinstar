About Qwinstar

Qwinstar is the largest servicer of check processing equipment, bank branch peripherals, and cash automation technology for financial institutions in the U.S. and Canada. Qwinstar is also a certified NCR Authorized Sorter Reseller and Repair Provider. With technicians located in over 75 metropolitan areas, Qwinstar’s support is unmatched in the industry.

Our diagnostic software and multi-vendor training allows our technicians to provide specialized 24/7 service on a variety of platforms such as IBM, NCR, Burroughs, Glory, and IBML. Our inventory is stocked with the industry’s largest stock of parts, ink, and fluid needed to get your business up and running as fast as possible.

We work with financial institutions, banks, retailers, cash in transit, and gaming companies to maintain and service their equipment. We pride ourselves on our preventative-maintenance focus and rigorous technician training programs in order to provide the greatest value to our clients.
